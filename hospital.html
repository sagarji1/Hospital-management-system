Emergency Medicine

A hospital must have a well-equipped emergency department capable of handling various medical emergencies, such as trauma cases, heart attacks, strokes, and other critical conditions.

Critical care services for patients with severe and life-threatening conditions, requiring continuous monitoring and specialized interventions.

Cardiology

Diagnosis and treatment of heart-related conditions, including cardiac surgery and interventional cardiology procedures.

Neurology

Specialized care for disorders of the nervous system, including neurological examinations, diagnostics, and treatment for conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, and neurodegenerative diseases.
